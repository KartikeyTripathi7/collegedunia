About Us

Collegedunia is an educational portal that provides information on colleges, competitive entrance exams, and courses in India and abroad. It was founded on May 5, 2014, by Sahil Chalana and is based in New Delhi, India. The website serves as a comprehensive platform for students seeking details about various educational institutions and programs.

Our philosophy revolves around empowering students with information to make informed decisions regarding their education and career paths. We aim to simplify the college search process by offering insights, reviews, and rankings of educational institutions.
